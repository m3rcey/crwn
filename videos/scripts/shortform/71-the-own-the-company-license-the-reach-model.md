## A WHOLE WAVE IS BUILDING THE LABEL FIRST

**CAPTION:**

Build The Company. Rent The Reach. Keep The Catalog.

Link in bio to own the company your music runs through with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

---

**SCRIPT:**

There's a model spreading right now. Build your own company first. Rent the major's reach second. Never hand over the catalog. Watch how it works.

Yall still think the only door is sign to a major and pray. There's a whole other door, and people are walking through it.

Burna Boy's setup isn't a one-off. His Spaceship Collective was built with one stated purpose, in writing: help artists keep creative control and own their catalogs. That's not a vibe. That's the design. The artist owns the label. The label owns the recordings. A major gets brought in for reach, on a deal, not as the owner.

A growing wave of artists are running this same order. Company first. Ownership locked. Then a partnership with a big company for distribution and reach that they can walk away from later.

The old way, you gave up the catalog to get the reach, forever. This way, you keep the catalog and rent the reach for a while.

You don't have to be Burna Boy to copy the order. The order is the part you can steal. Build the thing that's yours, then let the big reach come to it.

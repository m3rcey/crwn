## AN ALGORITHM PICKS WHO SEES YOUR POST. NOT YOU.

**CAPTION:**

A Post Is A Maybe. A Text Is A Sure Thing.

Link in bio to text your fans directly instead of begging the feed with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

---

**SCRIPT:**

You post to 50,000 followers and 800 see it. Watch who actually decided that.

Yall think posting to your page means reaching your page. You're renting a megaphone with a mute button somebody else holds.

When you post, the app decides how many of your own followers it even shows it to. Could be 2 percent. Could be less. You built that audience, but the feed stands between you and them and takes a toll every single time. New drop, big news, a show tonight, the algorithm still gets to decide if your people even find out.

Now a text message. You type it, you hit send, it lands. No algorithm in the middle deciding who's worthy. Nobody throttling it. The fan who gave you their number gets the message, period.

That's the whole difference. A post is a request the platform might grant. A text is a delivery you control. One you rent and beg for reach. The other one actually arrives.

Stop pouring everything into a channel that filters you to your own fans. The people who'd answer a text from you are worth more than ten times the people who might scroll past your post.

## SOMEONE TAKES THE BIGGER HALF OF YOUR BIGGEST HIT

**CAPTION:**

A Nickel On The Dollar vs The Bigger Half Of Everything

Link in bio to keep about ninety cents of every dollar with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

---

**SCRIPT:**

Picture your single biggest song. The one that pays for years. Now watch who actually keeps the money it makes.

Yall picture the hit. You never picture who's standing next to the money when it lands.

Run a clean dollar through the old way. A fan spends a dollar on your biggest record. By the time the label takes its majority, the distributor takes its piece, and recoupment pulls its part back first, the bigger half of that dollar is gone before it reaches you. On your best song. The one that's supposed to set you up. You walk away with the smaller half of your own peak.

Now run the same dollar a different way. A fan spends a dollar going straight to you. The platform takes a few cents to keep the lights on, single digits, a nickel on the dollar. You keep about ninety. Not on a mystery statement a year later. The day it lands.

That's the whole thing. It was never how big the hit was. It was who got the bigger half of it. On your peak record, one road leaves you the smaller piece and one road leaves you almost all of it.

Pick the road where the dollar mostly stays yours. The size of the song only matters if you keep what it makes.

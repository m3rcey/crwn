## BROCKHAMPTON, THE BAND A FORUM BUILT

**CAPTION:**

This Is How You Build A Band From A Forum

Link in bio to give your fans one room to find each other and build your world with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

---

**SCRIPT:**

BROCKHAMPTON started with one post on a stranger's forum, and that post ended somewhere most bands never reach. They call themselves a boy band. Around 2010 a kid named Kevin Abstract logged onto a Kanye West fan forum called KanyeToThe. He aint post a song. He posted a question. "Anybody wanna make a band?" Strangers answered. Other fans in that same room. They linked up, rebranded as BROCKHAMPTON in 2015, and started droppin. The fans wasnt the audience. The fans was the group. Most artists treat fans like a crowd you perform at. These fans wasnt no crowd. They was the band. So a band that strangers on a fan forum put together, no label scoutin em, no machine behind em, they kept goin till 2018. That year they dropped an album called Iridescence. It went straight to number one, the only number one they ever got.

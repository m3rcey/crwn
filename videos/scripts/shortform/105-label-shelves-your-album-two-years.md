## WHEN THE LABEL SITS ON YOUR ALBUM FOR TWO YEARS

**CAPTION:**

Would Your Fans Wait Two Years

Link in bio to reach your fans directly so a shelved release never freezes you with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

**SCRIPT:**

What happens when your album sits on a shelf for two years? Lets find out. It happens way more than labels admit. They sign you, then they sit on it. The single dont got a date. The fans start asking and you got no answer for em. Two years is long enough for a fanbase to forget you. Unless you can still reach em yourself. A text. An email. A post they actually see. Thats the only thing keeping em warm while the label sits there. This what nobody tells the new artist. The shelf aint even the worst part. The worst part is having no way to talk to your own people while you wait. If you own the line to your fans, the label sitting still cant freeze you. They can delay the album. They cant delay the relationship. Yea they can lock up the release. But they cant lock up the people who already love you.

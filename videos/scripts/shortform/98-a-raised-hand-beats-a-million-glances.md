A Million Glances Or A Few Thousand Raised Hands

**SCRIPT:**

A billboard gets a million glances. A link gets the few thousand people who already chose you. Watch why the small number wins.

Yall chase reach. Reach is the number that looks big and does nothing.

Think about who a billboard actually hits. People driving by who didn't ask, don't care, and forget it in three seconds. You pay for a million eyeballs and almost none of them were ever going to play your song. Big number, no intent behind it. That's most of what reach is. Strangers glancing.

Now think about who opens a link you send. Somebody who already followed you. Already decided. They didn't glance, they raised their hand. A few thousand people who chose you will do more for your release than a million who never will.

That's the part that flips it. It was never about how many people saw it. It was how many of them already wanted it. A raised hand is worth more than a glance, every time, and you can reach the raised hands directly for nothing.

Stop buying glances. Go straight to the people who already chose you. That number is smaller and it's the only one that pays.

**CAPTION:**

Link in bio to catch the fan slipping before they're gone with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

**SCRIPT:**

Right now you find out a fan left after they're already gone. What if something told you the day before? Watch the difference that one thing makes.

Yall react to people leaving. By then it's a goodbye, not a save.

Think about how it works for you now. A fan stops listening, stops opening, stops showing up. You don't notice for weeks. You only clock it when the numbers already dropped and they're long gone. You're reading a death certificate, not a warning.

A label never had this either, by the way. They had millions of strangers and a spreadsheet. They couldn't tell you which specific fan was cooling off, because they never knew your fans as people in the first place. Nobody had that list. Ever.

Now picture the flip. Something watches the patterns and pings you, this fan is slipping, reach them now. Before they cancel. While there's still a person on the other end who can be pulled back with one message.

That's the whole game. A save is only possible before the goodbye. You can't win back somebody you didn't know was leaving. The artists who keep their people are the ones who see it coming.

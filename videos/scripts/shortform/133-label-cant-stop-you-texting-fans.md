## A LABEL CAN STOP YOUR ALBUM, NOT YOUR FANS

**CAPTION:**

What They Own Vs What They Cant

Link in bio to hold a direct line to your fans the label can never touch with CRWN. Free to start at thecrwn.app.

**SCRIPT:**

This is what a label can do to you. And this is the one thing it cant touch. A label got a lot of power. It can shelf ya album. It can sit on ya single for two years. It can drop you and freeze ya whole catalog. It can decide you aint a priority no more. All of that, they can do. But here go the line they cant cross. They cant stop you from texting the people who love your music. They dont own ya phone. They dont own ya fans numbers. They dont own that relationship. If you got a direct line to ya fans, the label controls the music but not the bond. Now think about which one actually matters. They can lock up every song you made. But the bond with ya fans stays yours. Yea the label can freeze everything with your name on it. But they cant freeze the people who already ride for you.

**TOPIC:** On Kanye West's album Bully (March 28, 2026, Gamma), James Blake produced the closer "This One Here" from a 2022 Ye freestyle. Days after release Blake publicly asked for his producer credit to be removed, saying the released version "isn't what I created" and that he won't be credited on music he can't control (fans clocked AI vocals dropped over his work, on an album Ye had publicly promised had "NO AI").

Mac Miller did sign a major deal. But look at when, and look at what he signed as. That order changes everything. Watch.

That's the difference. He didn't walk in begging off one buzzing song. He walked in already a winner, with his own company, and let the major pay him for access to what he built.

Most artists sign hoping the label makes them. He signed after he already made himself, as a label, not a kid. Same Warner logo other people get owned under. He sat across the table as an owner.

Build the leverage first. Then the deal is something you negotiate, not something you pray for.

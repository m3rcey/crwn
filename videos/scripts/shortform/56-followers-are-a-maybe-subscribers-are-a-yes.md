Your Follower Count Is A Guess. Your Subscriber Count Isn't.

Yall flexing a follower count like it's money. Watch what it really is.

A follower clicked one button one time and moved on. They might see your next post. The app decides, not them. They might not even remember your name in a week. That's not a fan. That's a maybe the algorithm is holding hostage.

A subscriber did something different. They put a card on file and chose to keep paying you. That's not a maybe. That's a yes, in writing, every month, til they say stop. One you have to beg an app to show. The other one already raised their hand and reached in their pocket.

You can have 50,000 maybes and 0 dollars. You can have 80 yeses and rent covered. The big number feels good. The small number eats.

Stop counting who followed. Start counting who said yes with money. One is a vanity score. The other is a business.

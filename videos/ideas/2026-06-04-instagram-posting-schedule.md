HOW IT WORKS

Posting days: Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday.

Launch (Week 1):
  Thu Jun 4 - Personal "big announcement coming" TEASER, AND the CRWN schedule
              starts the same day (video 1 + carousel + repost). No reaction
              yet (nothing aired before it).
  Fri Jun 5 - Personal ANNOUNCEMENT post, plus the normal 4 machine posts.

Every posting day, the machine is 4 posts:
  1. CRWN video - next video in inventory order.
  2. CRWN carousel - next made carousel, in order (each is for a video that airs
     later or has not been filmed yet).
  3. Personal reaction video - reacts to the prior posting day's CRWN video.
  4. Personal carousel - the same carousel reposted a little later.

Labels below are number + slug so you grab the right file. Video files live in
videos/scripts/shortform. Carousel folders live in the Carousel Posts folder in
nano banana output.
